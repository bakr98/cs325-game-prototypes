    • Space Time Continuum (Iteration 2)

    • Author: Bakr Marouf
    • Originality: The X-eltors, your people's energy source have been stolen from you and spread across the universe. Journey across several galaxies and planets solving different kinds of puzzles from space puzzles in your ship to platforming puzzles like never before with a split mechanic when you land on specific planets.
    • Technical Merit: For this iteration, the synchroniously moving background elements in the galaxy and a panoramic background. The area set up is meant to serve as a dynamic level select and puzzle in finding the X-eltors (count displayed at all times) as well as keeping track of your health and charge of your ship. There are complete planet levels available for play in which the player can shift from space mode to a platformer puzzle mode and can leave the level when they finish the level and reach their rocket ship at the end of the level.
    • Prototype Postmortem: I learned how to create various collisions, a swarm of objects moving in different directions, and keeping track of various resources such as health and the X-eltors throughout scenes, I set up the platformer/maze and puzzle section of the game and this can be seen in the planet levels linked. I plan to polish this game more in the future and possibly release a polished product on the Android Play Store, iOS App Store, or simply online with all the levels ready and a final boss before beating the game itself.
